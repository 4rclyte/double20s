Double 20s

This dice game is played with two d20s. Roll your dice and add the result.

If you get under a 25 you lose your ante. On a 25 or higher you win!

If you happen to get an identical pair or match the ones digit(ex 2 & 12), you keep rolling!

On an identical match(4 & 4, etc) you add an additional 10 to your score.

Over 100 million simulated games the house is favored to win about 2.8% of the money at stake.

The legend below tells you the payout depending on your final result! Good luck!
