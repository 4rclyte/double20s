Jackpot or Gans? Fortunorgans? Name in progress...

This dice game is played with two d20s. The standard ante is 10g per play.

Roll your dice and add the result. If you get under a 26 you lose your ante.

However if you happen to get an identical pair or match the ones digit, you keep rolling!

On an identical match (4 and 4, etc) you add an additional 10 to your score.

Over 10 million simulated games the house is favored to win 1.1% of the money at stake.

The legend below tells you the payout depending on your final result! Good luck!

Score Payout
3 EPIC FAIL
4-25 Lose your bet
26-39 Win bet amount
40-99 Win bet * ((rollTotal / 10) - 1)
100+ Win bet * ((rollTotal / 9) * timesrolled)
